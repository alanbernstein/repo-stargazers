Who starred my repo?

Find out who are top 10 most important* users who starred your GitHub repo

* HOW IT WORKS: This simple app looks who starred your repo and counts how many stars have their own repos. All this data are taken from GitHub Archive project which might not be 100% accurate, but it is enough to provide us with rought idea who are the most important users starring your repo.

This app is open sourced and released under MIT license. If you want to improve the algorithm, you can find the source code on GitHub, obivously :)
